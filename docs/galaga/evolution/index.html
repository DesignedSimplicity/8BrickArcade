Evolution

Into the 4th dimension

Iterative design has evolved these simple pixelated sprites into imaginative 4th dimensional projections.


The first generation were simple arrangements of LEGO tiles on baseplates.

Extruding depth from them created pseudo-3D models called Flat Landers.

Escaping the constraint of the baseplate allowed for a more complex design.

Introducing hinges allows for movement between the two animation states.
